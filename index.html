About Clarity

Clarity is an AI-powered companion designed to provide a safe and supportive space for you to share your thoughts and feelings. Our goal is to help you gain clarity of thought through empathetic conversation.

Clarity is not a replacement for a licensed therapist, but can be a helpful tool for self-reflection and emotional processing.
